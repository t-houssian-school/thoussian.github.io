Site Purpose

To provide instantly the weather for you from the most historically accurate data sources. This is a site that is aimed towards those who want there weather reports more accurately and faster. It will provide the weather reports from multiple weather sources and average the data and allow a view into what other reports are saying. It will also rank the weather services by trackings whose predictions were accurate and who was off.
