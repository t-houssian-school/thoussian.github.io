Audience

This site is going to be targeted towards those who have more analytical minds and crave to know more about the data they are seeing and how it compares to other data. This website is going to do all the research for the differences between weather sites so those who really want to have more opinions on what the weather is going to be like can get them all in one place.

Scenarios

This site will apply to the following Scenarios:

- My weather app on my phone tells me it has a 30% chance of snow in the morning, but my friends phone says it has a 60% chance. Which one should I believe?

- I want to go skiing Tuesday at Kelly Canyon but only if it snows. How certain can I be that it will snow?

- I am going to have to snow blow in the morning at 8 AM if it snows before then, but I only want to get up if I know for sure it will snow that early.

- I plan to put new tires on my car and can pay a little more at one shop to get them on this week but if I wait then I can pay less at another shop. I need to know though if it will rain next week because I commute a lot and need to be safe on the roads.

- My bathroom flooded because of the great storm we have. I need to know how soon to get the roof fixed before the next storm but I need to know what a broad range of experts say on when the next storm will hit.

Personas

Jim Perfecto

Jim Perfecto is the principal of a school in Austin Texas. The school is on the outskirts of the city, about an hour from downtown and has about 400 students. The weather historically tends to be very crazy and shifts all the time. Sometimes the roads get very dangerous and he has to know when to delay or close the school so the students stay safe. But he doesn't want to cancel school if the weather really won't be too bad.

John Smith

John Smith is an outdoors enthusiast. He mountain bikes, hikes, snow skis, water skis, snowmobiles, motorbikes, mountain climbs, goes on multi-day treks, kayaks, and pretty much anything else outdoors. He is tired of having unpredictable weather strike and needs to know what data is reliable and what isn't for weather. He doesn't want to get caught in another lightning storm while on the summit of a mountain.
