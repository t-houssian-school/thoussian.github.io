Full-Time Temple Missionary

Serving as a full-time temple missionary and looking for your home away from home? Look no further than Temple Inn & Suites' Missionary Suites! We provide you a fully furnished aparment, that is simple, clean, and comfortable. You can pay month by month, year by year, or even upfront for the duration of your mission! You will be right accross the street from the temple and will have full access to the normal hotel amenities and special services. For any other questions please contact us!
