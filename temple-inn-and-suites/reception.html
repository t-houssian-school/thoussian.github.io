Reception Services

Setup

We have two rooms for wedding receptions each equiped with up to 20 tables, 150 chairs, a kitchen, decorations, eating and serving utensils, speakers, microphones, and smooth lighting to set the perfect mood for your wedding! You can opt to personally do the Setup or for only a small charge have us do it for you!

Food Options

  • Mexican Fiesta
  • - Includes Tacos, Flautas, Sopes, Horchata, and Fresh Fruits
  • Chinese Buffet
  • - Includes Orange Chicken, Mongolian Beef, Coconut Shrimp, Wontons, Egg Rolls, Sushi, and Fortune Cookies.
  • Italian Feast
  • - Includes Pizza, Four Chees Pasta, Fresh Sourdough Bread, Italian Sodas and Gelato.

Service

We can help out with as much or as little as you want with the wedding including serving food, setup, take down, and even dj. If there is anything else you would like us to do just let us know!
